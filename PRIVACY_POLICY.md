# Privacy Policy for EmberMate

**Last Updated**: January 3, 2025

## Introduction

EmberMate ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application EmberMate (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use EmberMate, you may voluntarily provide the following information:

**Health Information:**
- Medication names, dosages, and schedules
- Appointment details (doctor names, specialties, dates, locations)
- Patient information (name, date of birth, medical conditions)
- Vital signs (blood pressure, temperature, weight)
- Symptom logs and medical notes
- Care team member information
- Medical history and allergies

**Account Information:**
- Device identifiers (for local storage only)
- App settings and preferences
- Security settings (biometric/PIN preferences)

### Automatically Collected Information

**We DO NOT collect:**
- Analytics data
- Usage statistics
- Crash reports with personal information
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Camera/photo access (except when explicitly granted)
- Microphone access
- Any data transmitted to external servers

**We DO collect locally (on your device only):**
- App usage logs (for security audit trail)
- Last activity timestamp (for auto-lock)
- Local preferences and settings

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Data Storage and Processing

**LOCAL STORAGE ONLY:**
- All your health data is stored EXCLUSIVELY on your device
- We do NOT transmit your health information to any servers
- We do NOT share your data with third parties
- We do NOT use your data for marketing or advertising
- We do NOT sell your personal information

**Your data is used solely to:**
- Display your medication schedules and reminders
- Show your appointment calendar
- Track medication adherence
- Log symptoms and vital signs
- Generate care summaries for your personal use
- Provide security through authentication and audit logs

---

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Encryption:**
- AES-256 encryption for all sensitive data at rest
- Encryption keys stored in device secure keychain
- Optional password-encrypted backups

**Access Control:**
- Biometric authentication (Face ID/Touch ID/Fingerprint)
- PIN code protection
- Auto-lock after inactivity
- Failed attempt lockout

**Audit Logging:**
- Security event logging (local only)
- Automatic PII redaction in logs
- Tamper-evident audit trail

**Data Integrity:**
- Checksums for backup verification
- Data validation on all inputs
- Secure deletion when requested
