## üìù NOTES

**Estimated Review Times**:
- **App Store**: 24-48 hours (can be longer)
- **Google Play**: Few hours to 7 days (typically 1-3 days)

**Common Rejection Reasons**:
1. Incomplete app information
2. Privacy Policy issues
3. Crashes or bugs
4. Misleading screenshots
5. Insufficient app functionality
6. Privacy violations
7. Data safety disclosures incomplete

**Tips for Faster Approval**:
1. Provide clear review notes
2. Include demo account
3. Explain encryption usage
4. Highlight privacy features
5. Test thoroughly before submission
6. Respond quickly to review feedback
7. Be patient and professional
